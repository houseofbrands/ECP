Product e-Commerce Photography

In today's digital world, high-quality product photography is essential for brands looking to stand out in a crowded marketplace. Our expertise in product eCommerce photography, honed through years of experience in the fashion photography industry, enables us to capture the essence of your products and the identity of your brand.

all about the details

ECP photographers are experts in all lighting techniques and are always available to discuss your preferences. Our London based studios are fully equipped with the latest lighting carefully selected for any e-com production. 

Our extensive knowledge of both photography and fashion allows us to create insightful image content and identify upcoming trends to ensure we are always ahead of the curve.

Proven track record

We work with the most iconic and innovative fashion brands in the industry and we are committed to delivering the same level of expertise and quality for every client.

Product e-Commerce Photography FAQs

Get the full picture on our product e-commerce photography services with our frequently asked questions.

1. How does ECP ensure the quality of product eCommerce photography?

At ECP, we maintain high standards of quality for our product eCommerce photography by employing a team of seasoned professionals who use carefully honed techniques to deliver exceptional product photographs. We recognise that every brand has a unique vision and identity and we collaborate closely with our clients to create product images that align with their marketing goals, helping to elevate their digital presence and drive sales.

2. What is the turnaround time for ECP's product eCommerce photography services?

The turnaround time for our product eCommerce photography services depends on the scope and complexity of your project. We always strive to deliver high-quality, optimised images within a timeframe that meets your needs and expectations. We encourage you to discuss your specific requirements with our team and we will provide an estimated timeline for your project.

3. Can ECP provide different styles of product eCommerce photography?

Yes, at ECP, we can provide different styles of product eCommerce photography, such as flat lay, mannequin, and hanging. Our team can work with you to curate the ideal style for your product line, ensuring a cohesive and visually stunning representation of your brand.

4. Can ECP provide post-production services for eCommerce photography?

Absolutely. At ECP, we offer a full suite of post-production services for our digital content, including image retouching, colour correction, and background removal, ensuring each photograph is polished and adheres to your brand's aesthetic and is ready for your online store.

5. Can ECP provide on-model photography in addition to product photography?

Yes, at ECP, we can provide on-model photography in addition to product photography to help showcase your products in a real-world context, demonstrating how they would look on a person. Our team can collaborate with you to select the ideal model that aligns with your brand's vision and style, ensuring that the look and feel complement your product line. On-model photography is a critical pathway for our iconic clients to visualise their products. Learn more about our model ecommerce photography service here.

6. Can ECP shoot product photography for different types of fashion products?

Certainly. At ECP, we provide product photography services for various types of fashion products, including clothing, footwear, accessories, and more. Our team is flexible and can work with your specifications or collaborate with you to create a new visual representation of your brand that aligns with your unique style and aesthetic.
